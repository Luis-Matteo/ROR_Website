Sparanoid is a personal website about fine arts and web design.

About Tunghsiao Liu

Tunghsiao Liu (劉通曉), an undergraduate at Tianjin Academy of Fine Arts. You are welcome to ping me on Twitter or send correspondence to info@sparanoid.com.

The following are some of my projects.

  • 7-Zip L10n Project - An open-source file archiver. I maintain Chinese Simplified localization since 2004.
  • Quotmarks Replacer - A WordPress plugin disables wptexturize function that keeps all quotation marks and suspension points in half-width form.

Colophon
